# Nigerian Fish Sales Analysis (2019–2023)

Exploratory analysis of fish sales data from a Nigerian store, covering revenue trends, breakdowns by fish type (Catfish, Tilapia, etc.), condition (Dried/Smoked/etc.), customer locations (Lagos, Abuja, PH), types (Corporate/Retail), sales channels, payment methods, suppliers, and purchase frequency.

**Key Insights**:
- Total Revenue: ₦3,473,668 across 2019–2023.
- Top fish: Catfish (~26%), Tilapia (~26%).
- Customer Location: Port Harcourt leads (~35%).
- Sales Channel: Balanced In-store vs Online (~50% each).
- YoY Growth: Slight dip in 2022, strong recovery in 2023 (+12.84%).

## Project Goals
- Clean and explore transaction-level data.
- Identify revenue drivers, trends, and patterns.
- Prepare insights for business decisions (e.g., stock optimization, targeting high-value customers).

## Dataset
- **Source**: Synthetic/aggregated Nigerian fish store transactions (2019–2023).
- **File**: `nigeria_fish_store_data_2019_2023.xlsx` (~1,800+ rows in raw sheet).
- **Columns**: Date, Fish Type, Source Location, Fish Size/Condition, Price, Discounts, Quantity Sold, Total Sales, Customer Type/Location, etc.
- Sheets: Raw Data, Insights (summaries), Revenue Analysis (pivots), Dashboard placeholder.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (PivotTables, charts, slicers for breakdowns).
- Data cleaning: Handling dates, duplicates, formats.
- Visualizations: Bar/pie charts for categories, line charts for monthly/YoY trends.

## How to Explore
1. Download the repo.
2. Open `nigeria_fish_store_data_2019_2023.xlsx` in Excel.
3. Check **Insights** sheet for quick summaries.
4. Use **Revenue Analysis** pivots to filter/interact.
5. Replicate: Refresh pivots or add your own slicers.
